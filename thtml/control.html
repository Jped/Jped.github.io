Control.

I am not sure if control of every detail in life is necessary. Obviously I would feel more safe and comfortable if I had full control,
but I feel that takes away from the experience of life.

Anyhow, I would like to write about self-control with social interactions. Something which I think is very important and that I am very bad at.

What I mean by this is the ability to control what I say to people and how I react to certain situations.

I tend to say things that I am not supposed too to certain people. In all honestly, I do not think that is such a big issue. I think the bigger
issue lies in my impatience in certain group situations. Stemming from my desire for command, I get paranoid (this is the wrong word...) when things
dont go the way i want them to go.

This is ridiculous and immature. Just control your shit man.
