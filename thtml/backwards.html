backwards

2018-05-09

Hope is a fascinating thing.
Without it there is no drive. Why should I push hard if there is no vision of something bigger and better in the future?
Yet at a certain point there is a questing of the hope. Maybe the hope is na√Øve maybe it does not lead to anything.
Tame Imapla has this song "feels like we only go backwards." The song is as the name suggests, and is the feeling I am currently feeling.

On paper, I have lots of hope. On paper, I have lots of aspirations. On paper I know what I need to do, and what I need to improve.
But in real life, every time I feel like I have crossed off one of those tasks, I realize that I really have not finished any of the previous task.
Maybe this is due to my initial naivety, maybe I assumed solving the initial task was simple, or maybe I improved at that moment, just to fall back into my
old habits.

It is hard to push forward when it is not clear which direction you are moving in. Was the past Jonathan Pedoeem better than the current one? Maybe in some ways,
but he sure as hell was not better overall. The past Jonathan has not gone through all the experiences current Jonathan has gone through.

So whats the deal. What do I do? How do I proceed?
Maybe I expect to much out of myself too quickly. I am willing to accept that. But giving up completely seems like throwing in the hat too early. I need to just keep on
iterating and moving forward. I will trust that there will be improvement and that I will move forward, otherwise things are a little too grim.
