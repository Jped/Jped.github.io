Doubts.

Personal doubt is something I feel often. Its a tense feeling, sort of like a fence around the chest.
It hurts, and it weighs me down.

I am trying to think objectively here, is there any use to it?

It just makes me feel like shit.

Honestly, I think the key is to just keep pushing. Pick a path and just go. Move quickly.

If it turns out to be a bad move, re-correct, dont waver to much to think too much.

Yet, I feel this is easier said than done.
